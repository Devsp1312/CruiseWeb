Core Principles

Pack Smart

  • Mix and match pieces
  • Layer-friendly options
  • Wrinkle-resistant fabrics

Climate Ready

  • Lightweight, breathable fabrics
  • Quick-dry materials

Understanding MSC Dress Codes üìã

Casual & Comfortable:

  • Shorts, t-shirts, polos
  • Sundresses, casual skirts
  • Swimwear (pool/beach areas only)
  • Athletic wear (gym/sports only)

Note: Cover-ups required when walking through indoor areas

Smart Casual:

  • Collared shirts
  • Dress pants or chinos
  • Smart dresses or skirts
  • Closed-toe shoes

Not Allowed: Shorts, flip-flops, tank tops, athletic wear

Formal Attire:

  • Dark suit with tie
  • Blazer with dress pants & tie
  • Formal dress shoes
  • Tuxedo (optional)
